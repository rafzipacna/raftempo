Welcome to the Raftempo Chorale!

About Us

The Raftempo Chorale is a vibrant community of music lovers dedicated to sharing the joy of singing. We welcome members of all skill levels and backgrounds.

Membership Information

If you are interested in joining us, here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Weekly rehearsals on Wednesdays from 7 PM to 9 PM.
  • Annual membership fee: $50.
  • Performances throughout the year, including concerts and community events.
  • We accept singers of all voice parts: Soprano, Alto, Tenor, Bass.

How to Join

If you would like to become a member, please reach out to us via email at info@raftempochorale.org. We look forward to welcoming you to our community!
